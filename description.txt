Adds completionist achievements to the in-game list.

Rules:
- No Journey Mode
- No Celebrationmk10 Seed

Items that are exclusive to difficulties and special seeds are excluded, so that all achievements can be obtained in a normal playthrough. I will publish other achievement mods that focus on specific difficulties and special seeds.

Contribute language localization here: https://github.com/timenoe/Terraria-CompletionistAchievements
